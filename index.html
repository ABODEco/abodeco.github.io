Idea

A control panel that is remotely connected to all powerpoint switches in a household. Each having the ability to remotely turn each switch on or off at the press of a button. Can be connected via classic signal such as a remote control or connected to the house wifi.

Development

The development of these smart powerpoints and smart hub can consist of tools already developed today. Therefore meaning development of this project can be done with todays technology. Said technology consists of a infared radiation signaling (the form of communication a remote has with a tv) and then standard 2.4 gigahertz frequencies for a wifi centred method.

Production

Production of this project would come in the form of mass production seeing as countless smart powerpoints would have to be made and many smart hubs. Production can be autonomous to ensure supply meets demand.

Distribution

The powerpoints and smart hub will be installed in every new house after released to the public. Each new house will require the need to have smart powerpoints over old original ones. At the same time the option to have your current powerpoints replaced with smart powerpoints will be open to the public.

Installation

Installation will be carried out by an electrician when a new household is being built. When a customer buys the product for their already existing house, the customer can install everything themselves or an option for an electrician to come out to install the smart huband smart powerpoints will also be available.

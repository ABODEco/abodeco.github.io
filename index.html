Russel

I am an Australian born Sri Lankan so naturally I am an avid cricket fan and love a good curry. I speak Sinhalese at home and have previously studied at Monash University, I was studying a Science degree but couldn’t envision myself in the field within the next ten years, I was after a bit more diversity and change, Information Technology stood out as I found myself intrigued by my friends working on their assignments. The coding aspects and the all round skills they were doing on tasks, ranging from coding to management to implementation seemed like a field with a lot of opportunities so naturally I figured there is a higher chance that I will be able to find something that I love doing, so I enrolled in a couple IT units where I learnt the basics of Java, HTML and a few other languages and I haven’t looked back since. I saw RMIT as an opportunity to follow that dream and set myself on my way. I feel goals are not as important as the process, I mean you may get to do something you absolutely love but if it was ten years of painstaking effort, I’d rather not, I’d rather the process be as enjoyable as the end product.
